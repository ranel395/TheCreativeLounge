What We Offer

Services

Here are the services we offer.

Live Virtual Cooking Sessions

Live virtual cooking sessions are interactive online classes where customers cook alongside a professional instructor in real time. Participants follow step by step guidance to prepare a specific recipe while learning cooking techniques and tips. These sessions create an engaging and social experience that allows customers to improve their cooking skills from the comfort of their homes.

Meal Prep Coaching

Meal prep coaching provides customers with guidance on planning, preparing, and organizing meals for the week. Through virtual sessions or instructional content, customers learn how to save time, eat healthier, and reduce food waste by preparing meals in advance. The service helps individuals and families create efficient meal routines that fit their schedules and dietary needs.

Loyalty Rewards Program

The loyalty rewards program encourages repeat customers by offering points, discounts, or special perks for purchases and participation in classes. Customers can earn rewards when they buy kits, attend virtual sessions, or subscribe to monthly experiences. This program helps build long term customer relationships and increases engagement with the brand.

Private Event Experiences

Private event experiences allow groups to book customized virtual cooking or craft sessions for special occasions. These events may include birthday celebrations, corporate team building activities, family gatherings, or holiday parties. Each event is guided by an instructor and provides a fun, interactive experience where participants can learn, create, and connect together online.

Products

Find the perfect kit to experience.
Craft Kits
Craft kits are all in one DIY packages that include the materials, tools, and instructions needed to complete creative projects at home. These kits may include activities such as candle making, painting, jewelry making, or seasonal crafts. Each kit is designed to be beginner friendly and provides a guided experience that allows customers to learn new skills while enjoying a fun and relaxing activity.
Cooking Kits
Cooking kits provide customers with pre-measured ingredients, recipes, and step by step instructions to prepare meals at home. These kits may include themed experiences such as family dinner kits, date night meals, or international cuisine. Cooking kits make it easier for customers to enjoy a cooking experience without needing to shop for ingredients or plan recipes.
Subscription Kits
Subscription kits are recurring monthly packages that deliver new craft or cooking experiences directly to customers. Each month, subscribers receive a curated kit with materials or ingredients, instructions, and access to optional virtual classes or tutorials. This model encourages ongoing engagement, convenience, and a continuous creative or culinary experience for customers.
